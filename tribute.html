Nimea Lorenshield

Goblin Slayer

Nimea in full ebony battleplate, wielding the elven mace Fyregeld.

Nimea's Timeline:

  • 637 - Nimea Lorenshield is born to human and elven parentage.
  • 645 - Nimea begins to display Clerical affinity, and is trained in the ways of the light.
  • 651 - Nimea's village is attacked by tribe of marauding goblins and her mother is slain defending the holy temple. Nimea vows vengeance as the temple is burnt to the ground.
  • 655 - On her 18th birthday, Nimea ventures forth after being gifted with the elven mace Fyregeld, gifted to her from the elven vaults by the village elders.
  • 656 - Nimea slays her first goblin warband, led by the chieftain Skrotsnik. His head is handed to City Guards, yet she refuses payment - "Justice is payment enough".
  • 659 - Bringing low the foul machinations of the Cult of the Damned with the aid of a human rogue by the name of Amellis, she spends the night in his company. When she wakes the following morning, her gold and valuables have been thieved by the very knave who had helped her the day before. Amellis is added to her list of foes.
  • 660 - Nimea tracks the goblin warlord Rotgut to his lair under the Willowsmoke Mountains. The path into the gloomy dark is long and treacherous...

"For as long as Sol rises in the East each day will I smite every damnable goblin I find."

- Nimea Lorenshield
